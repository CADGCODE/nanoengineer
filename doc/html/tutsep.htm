How to separate a chunk into two separate chunks.

1. Choose Select Atoms mode from context menu or the toolbar.

2. Use a bounding box or lasso selection to select the section of atoms from the Chunk you wish to separate.

3. Once the section of the Chunk is selected, select the separate icon from the Modify toolbar.

  • Now the item can be seen as two separate parts.
  • In Move Chunk mode the objects can be moved separately.

4. Double click the screen to enter Move mode.

5. Once in Move Chunk mode, use the LMB to drag the seperated parts to their new location.
